The IITM-ECR Hub

“Built right, from the ground up.​”

Background and Rationale


The IITM-ECR Hub is proposed to support Early Career Researchers (ECRs) navigating the complex, interdisciplinary field of Earth System Science. By offering structured onboarding, cross-training, and regular discussions, the hub aims to ease the learning curve for researchers from diverse backgrounds and promote collaboration in areas like modeling, prediction, and outreach. It will foster a vibrant community of young scientists at IITM, encourage innovation through shared learning, and build strong linkages with global ECR networks. The hub also envisions future expansion across MoES institutes to create a nationally integrated ECR ecosystem.
